HOW I MEET YOUR MOM

HOMELAND

Homeland is an Emmy Award-winning thriller series that ran from 2011 to 2020 on Showtime. The show is about a CIA officer and a decorated Marine Sergeant who navigate lies, suspicion, and desire.

HART DIXIE

The series, created by Leila Gerstein, stars Rachel Bilson as Dr. Zoe Hart, a New Yorker who, after her dreams of becoming a heart surgeon fall apart, accepts an offer to work as a general practitioner in the fictional Gulf Coast town of Bluebell, Alabama. Hart of Dixie premiered on September 26, 2011.

THE ENGLISH

2 BROKE GIRLS

2 Broke Girls is a comedy series about two young women who work as waitresses at a diner and develop an unlikely friendship. The show ran from 2011-2017 and has 138 episodes over six seasons.

SUITS

Suits is a comedy-drama TV series that ran from 2011 to 2019. The show is about a college dropout with a photographic memory, Mike Ross (Patrick J. Adams), who works as an associate for a successful New York City corporate lawyer, Harvey Specter (Gabriel Macht), even though he's never attended law school.

PRISON BREAK

Prison Break is a TV series that ran from 2005-2017 and is about two brothers, Lincoln Burrows (Dominic Purcell) and Michael Scofield (Wentworth Miller), who are involved in a complex plan to break Lincoln out of prison

FRIENDS

Friends is a comedy-romance TV show that ran from 1994-2004 and follows the lives of six friends in their 20s and 30s living in New York City's Greenwich Village. The show was created by David Crane and Marta Kauffman and stars Jennifer Aniston, Courteney Cox, Lisa Kudrow, Matt LeBlanc, Matthew Perry, and David Schwimmer

MOVIES

MONGOL

Mongol is a 2008 film that tells the story of Genghis Khan's early life, from his birth in 1162 as Temudgin to his rise as a legendary conqueror. The film is based on scholarly accounts and was directed by Sergei Bodrov. It stars Tadanobu Asano as Temudgin, Sun Hong Lei as Jamukha, and Khulan Chuluun as Borte.

JUMANJI

umanji is a 1995 family fantasy action movie about a young boy who discovers a magical board game that transports him to the jungles of Jumanji, where he remains for 26 years. Two children eventually free him from the game's spell. The movie is based on an award-winning children's book and stars Robin Williams, Kirsten Dunst, and Bonnie Hunt

AVATAR

Avatar is a 2009 action-adventure-fantasy film about a paraplegic Marine who travels to the alien planet Pandora to take part in a program that explores and exploits the planet's resources. The film's title refers to the genetically engineered human-Na'vi hybrids, called Avatars, that the humans use to interact with the Na'vi

DAMSEL

Damsel is a 2024 American dark fantasy film about a young woman, Elodie (Millie Bobby Brown), who agrees to marry a prince to save her people from death. However, she soon discovers that the prince has ulterior motives and that she is being used as a sacrifice to a dragon to repay an ancient debt. Elodie must then use her wits and bravery to survive and escape the cave where she is thrown with the dragon.

THE KING WARRIOR

Warrior King is a 2023 animated fantasy film about a young Chinese boy who is destined to become a great king. The movie is based on the Chinese legend of King Gesar, which is considered a literary classic in Central Asia. The story has been passed down through oral tradition for over 1,000 years and has been adapted into literature, poetry, and stage plays.

CIVIL WAR

Civil War is a 2024 movie directed by Alex Garland that follows a group of journalists during a civil war in the United States. The journalists, led by Kirsten Dunst's character Lee Smith, travel from New York City to Washington, D.C. to interview the President before rebels take over the capital. Their journey takes them through conflict zones and refugee camps, showing the human cost of the war.

CURSE OF THE NUN

Curse of the Nun is a 2019 horror, mystery, and thriller film about a young woman who is looking forward to moving to a new home, but the spirit of a deranged nun wants her to stay where she is. As the battle wages on, we learn more of sister Catherine's sinister motivations and Anna's troubled past.

SINISTER

Sinister is a 2012 horror, mystery, and thriller film about a true-crime writer who moves his family into a house where a murder took place and discovers a box of Super 8 home movies that may be connected to the case. The movie stars Ethan Hawke as Ellison Oswalt, a writer who's struggling to find success and is desperate for a hit.

BLACK ADAM

Black Adam is a 2022 DC Extended Universe (DCEU) movie about an ancient superhuman who is freed from his imprisonment to fight crime and protect the world

SPORTS

BIG SHORT

Big Shot is a Disney+ sports drama series that ran from 2021-2022. The show is about a college basketball coach who is fired after a temper tantrum during a game that injures a referee. He then moves to California to coach the girls' basketball team at Westbrook School, an elite all-girls private high school.

THE ENGLISH GAME

Two 19th-century footballers on opposite sides of a class divide navigate professional and personal turmoil to change the game — and England — forever. Watch all you want. This richly detailed ensemble drama comes from "Downton Abbey" creator and "Gosford Park" writer Julian Fellowes.

SPORTS NIGHT

Sports Night is an American sitcom that aired from 1998-2000 about the behind-the-scenes drama of a fictional sports news show called Sports Night. The show follows the creative staff as they navigate friendships, ethical issues, and network pressure while trying to produce a good show.

THE LAST DANCE

The Last Dance is a 10-part ESPN docuseries about the Chicago Bulls' final six championship seasons, with a focus on Michael Jordan and his role in forming one of the greatest dynasties in basketball history. The series includes behind-the-scenes footage that was kept under seal for 22 years.

TED LASSO

Ted Lasso is a comedy-drama series that ran from 2020-2023 on Apple TV+. The show follows Ted Lasso, an American college football coach who is hired to manage AFC Richmond, a struggling English Premier League soccer team. Ted has no experience with soccer, but his optimistic leadership and enthusiasm unexpectedly lead to success.

SWAGGER

Swagger is a TV series that explores the world of youth basketball, and the players, their families, and coaches who navigate the fine line between dreams and ambition, and opportunism and corruption. The series follows a basketball prodigy who must overcome the odds against him and learn what it truly means to have swagger.

CRICKET

Cricket is a team sport involving a bat and ball played between two teams of eleven players each. The objective is to score more runs (points) than the opposing team. A match is divided into innings during which one team bats, two batter at a time, and the other team bowls.
